Game Ping Pong

Drag the paddle to hit the ball

Hit slowly or quickly to control the ball speed.

To get more spin, drag the paddle sideways after hitting the ball

Control

=> If you choose play with computer , you move mouse to move paddle

=> If you choose play with player ,P1 move with key W(Up) and S(Down), P2 move with key I(Up) and K(Down)
